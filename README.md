# Customer-churn-analysis
Using EXCEL
This project analyzes customers churn data to understand why customers leave and hpw retention can be improved.The analysis focuses on identifying behavioral patterns ,satisfaction levels and customer segments that are most at risk of churn with the goal of supporting data-driven retention strategies

The project is designed to reflect real-world business analysis where insights are used to guid decision-making rather than simple report metrics.

## Businesss Objectives
- Identify key drivers of customer churn
- understand which customer segments are most likely to leave
- Evaluate the relationship between satisfaction usage and churn
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.


  Data Scope
- Dataset includes customer demographic usage and satisfaction data
- Covers multiple customer segment across different tenure periods
- Analysis focuses on historical churn behaviour to idnetify patterns and risks.


## Key Metrics Analyzed
- Churn Rate and Retention Rate
- Customer Tenure
- Customer Stisfaction Score
- Usage and Engagement Indicators
- churn

## Key Insights
- Customers with lower satisfaction scores were sighnifcantly more likely to churn highlighting satisfaction as a primary churn driver
- Early tenure customers showed higher churn rates suggesting on board and early engagement gaps
- Certain customer segments consistently demonstrate higher churn indicating misalighnment between customer expectaton and service delivery.

## Recommendation
- Strengten onboarding and early engagement programs to reduce first period churn
- Proactively monitory satisfaction scores to identify at risk customers early.
- Develope targeted retention strategies for high- churn customer segments.


## Business Impact
  If applied in a real life business enviroment these insights could help reduce churn ,improve customer lifetime value and support more focused retenion efforts.
